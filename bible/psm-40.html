134:1 A Song of the going up.

134:1 （上行之诗。）耶和华的仆人，夜间站在耶和华殿中的，你们当称颂耶和华。

Give praise to the Lord, all you servants of the Lord, who take your places in the house of the Lord by night.

134:2 你们当向圣所举手，称颂耶和华。

134:2 Give praise to the Lord, lifting up your hands in his holy place.

134:3 愿造天地的耶和华，从锡安赐福给你们。

134:3 May the Lord, who made heaven and earth, send you blessing out of Zion,
