Wenzhou Aims to Remake Chinese Finance: Who Wants to Help?

温州金改招贤纳士 虚席以待可有人来

2012年06月22日12:52

（本文版权归道琼斯公司所有，未经许可不得翻译或转载。）

After generations operating in the shadows of China's financial system, providing credit to places the state-owned banks ignored, underground lenders in the coastal city of Wenzhou are finally going legit.

几代人以来，沿海城市温州的地下钱庄都是在中国金融系统的阴影下经营，在那些被国有银行忽略的角落经营业务。现在，它们终于合法了。

The need to transform the city's privately run loan shops into larger and more sophisticated financial institutions is attracting a wave of interest from professionals around the country, attracted by well paying jobs. The catch: The jobs are not in the private sector.

将温州私营钱庄变成更大更成熟金融机构的必要性引起了全国各地专业人士的兴趣──他们受到了高薪工作的吸引。不过吊诡的是，这些工作机会并不在这些私营钱庄。

In what, according to one academic, could be China's largest ever government-organized recruitment program, Wenzhou is looking to fill 108 positions, most of which are at state-owned enterprises and government finance institutions. According to Wenzhou Municipal Party Committee Organization Department the roles which include nine senior management positions and 13 middle-level positions at city-level financial institutions, 17 management positions at state-owned enterprises and 69 positions for regional banks, securities firms and insurance houses have attracted nearly a thousand applicants since the program was launched since late March.

Bloomberg News温州市五马街温州计划为108个工作岗位招聘人才，而多数岗位都是在国有企业和政府金融机构。一位学者说，这可能是中国有史以来由政府组织的最大规模的招聘行动。据温州市委组织部说，自从3月下旬推出这个计划以来，已经有接近1,000人提出申请。空缺岗位包括市级金融机构的九个高级管理职位和13个中级管理职位，国有企业的17个管理职位，以及区域性银行证券公司和保险机构的69个职位。

In a full page advertisement in the Economic Daily in mid-April, the city detailed benefits for many of the positions, including an annual salary of up to 1 million yuan ($158,730) for the deputy general manager of the Bank of Wenzhou Co Ltd.

在4月中旬《经济日报》上的一幅整版广告中，温州详细描述了很多岗位的待遇，其中温州银行股份有限公司(Bank of Wenzhou Co Ltd.)副行长一职年薪最高达到人民币100万元（合158,730美元）。

While the recruitment drive has drawn a lot of attention from the media and applicants alike, the question for many scholars and industry professionals is whether Wenzhou has got what it takes to attract the thousands of other finance sector professionals it needs.

虽然招聘计划引发了媒体和求职者的很大关注，但在很多学者和专业人士看来，温州还需要成千上万名其他类型的金融专业人士，有没有条件吸引这些人前来，才是问题所在。

Wenzhou's financial reform program kicked off in late March after slowing exports sparked a localized financial crisis, with small businesses defaulting on loans and local lenders reining in lending. The State Council, China's cabinet, gave the city the greenlight to legalize and develop private financing, professionalize asset management, and develop local capital markets in an effort to ensure the economic stability and sustainability of the southern metropolis.

温州金融改革计划是在3月下旬启动的。之前出口放缓引发了一场地方性的金融危机，小企业贷款违约，当地银行控制放贷。国务院批准温州赋予民间融资合法地位并促进民间融资发展，实现资产管理专业化，并发展本地资本市场，以确保这个南方大都市的经济稳定和可持续发展。

More than 50,000 people are already involved in the local financial industry, according to the website of the official People's Daily, but most of them are not professionally-trained. Moreover, the website says, this number is far from enough given the city aims for finance to be a 'pillar industry' by 2015.

据人民网说，温州金融行业已有5万余从业人员，但其中多数都没有接受过专业训练。另外该网还说，考虑到温州打算到2015年将金融业打造成“支柱产业”，这个数量远远不够。

Wenzhou Communist Party Secretary Chen Derong, speaking at a conference on financial industry reform on April 25th, said Wenzhou would have at least 100 micro-credit companies with 40 billion yuan in net assets by the end of 2013, adding to the current 28 micro-finance companies and 30 rural credit cooperatives, according to financial newspaper Shanghai Securities News.

财经媒体《上海证券报》(Shanghai Securities News)报道，温州市委书记陈德容在4月25日的金融业改革大会上发言时说，到2013年底，温州将至少有100家净资产在400亿元的小额信贷公司，而现在温州只有28个小额信贷公司和30个农村信用合作社。

Lilian Lin

或许，温州有关部门目前希望，初步招聘108名专业人士的计划日后会被证明是一个具有宿命意义的决定。在中国古典小说《水浒传》中，最初就有108位好汉在招募更大规模的军队与朝廷抗争之前在梁山聚义。问题是，今天的金融专业人士是会受到影响从而采取行动参与中国金融改革，还是会因要放弃北京和上海来到不那么国际化的温州而裹足不前。
